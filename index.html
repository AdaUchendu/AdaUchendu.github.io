--- layout: page title: Adaku Uchendu subtitle: I graduated from the University of Maryland Baltimore County with a B.S in Mathematics and a minor in Statistics in May 2018. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Information Sciences and Technology at Pennsylvania State University under the guidance of my Research Advisor, Dr. Dongwon Lee, an Associate professor at the University. My research interests are data mining, machine learning, and cyber-security. Specifically, I am interested in research that leverages data mining and machine learning techniques to solve issues that have security implications. Currently, I am working on a project that uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) techniques to distinguish chatbot-generated texts from human-generated texts with Dr. Dongwon Lee. This requires combining NLP and data mining tools with Python packages to build machine learning models to detect Human and Chatbot texts from the data. Furthermore, my goal is to find linguistic patterns that prove Human and Machine texts are in fact very different. use-site-title: true ---
